Brazilian Cheese Bread

This vegan Brazilian cheese bread (pao de queijo) is golden and crisp on the outside while stretchy, soft and fluffy on the inside - like little mochi clouds. Instead of dairy cheese, we use tapioca starch to create that signature pull and bouncy texture Brazil is famously obsessed with. They're salty, savory, snackable and kind of addictive in the most harmless way.

Total Time: 45 minutes

Yield: 5 servings x1

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Whisk boiling water and olive oil, then add to dry mix.
  4. Add mashed potato and mix until dough forms.
  5. Roll into balls and place on baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 32-35 minutes until bottoms are golden.
