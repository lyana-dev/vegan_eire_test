Blueberry Chocolate Cake

Base layer
  • 3 cups cashews (soaked 1 hr, rinsed)
  • ½ cup water
  • ¾ cup melted coconut oil
  • ½ cup light agave syrup
  • 15 tbsp cocoa powder
Cashew cream
  • 3 cups cashews (soaked 1 hr, rinsed)
  • ½ cup water
  • ¾ cup melted coconut oil
  • ½ cup light agave syrup
  • 15 tbsp cocoa powder
Method

Blend ingredients for cashew cream. If the cream is too thin, add a little coconut flour. Mix slowly and thoroughly. Place into a springform pan (approx. 20cm diameter).Put in the fridge. After three hours, remove from the fridge, pour cashew cream on top and sprinkle with blueberries.

Sesame Love

Ingredients
  • 500 ml tahini (room temp)
  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 2 cups coconut flour
  • ½ cup light agave syrup
  • ½ tsp Himalayan salt
  • 6 tbsp cocoa powder (divided)
  • 5 tbsp nut butter (divided)
  • 2 tbsp decaf instant coffee (dissolved)
  • 200 ml coconut cream (for light layer)
  • 2 tbsp coconut flour (for light layer)
  • 200 ml coconut cream (for dark layer)
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder (for dark layer)
  • 3 tbsp light agave syrup (for dark layer)
  • Peanuts + chocolate glaze (for topping)
Method
  1. Mix tahini, flours, agave, and salt then knead into uniform mass. Divide into 3 parts.
  2. Part 1: Add 3 tbsp cocoa, press into 20cm springform pan.
  3. Part 2: Add nut butter + dissolved coffee, spread over base.
  4. Part 3: Add cocoa + nut butter, spread on top.
  5. Light cream: Mix coconut cream + coconut flour.
  6. Dark cream: Mix coconut cream + cocoa + agave.
  7. Pour light cream, then dark cream over layers.
  8. Sprinkle peanuts, cover with chocolate glaze. Chill overnight.

Method

Put all the ingredients together in a jar and leave it overnight.

Banana Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 1 tablespoons light agave syrup
  • 1 teaspoon natural vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of organic cinamon
  • 1 teaspoon of ceremonial organic cacao
Method

Blend all ingredients Mix all together until smooth. Topped with fresh fruits of your choice.
